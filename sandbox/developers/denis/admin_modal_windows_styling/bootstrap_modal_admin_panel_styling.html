As some who generally wears very basic make-up, I wanted to look more dramatic on my wedding day, but still like "me". I initially wasn't even going to have my makeup done; but wanted a trial, after seeing pictures of her work. I would work with Betsy again , so glad I decided to have our make up professionally done. I highly recommend her !!! Thank you Betsy!!
