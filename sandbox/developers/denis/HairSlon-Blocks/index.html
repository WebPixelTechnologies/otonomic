Jasmine A. Cox

My 12 year old had her first dance and I wanted to get her a blow out for this special occassion. She was less than thrilled with the prospect and complained as we looked for a salon between the orthodontist and the Path Station. We came upon Carleo and I persuaded her to go it. Lisa had us wait for Martin. While my daughter was having her hair washed Martin came to me to ask what we wanted and I explained that she wanted some curls but nothing fancy. Martin said the magic words that relaxed me and made me feel so comfortable: that she would looks like her. Martin did a beautiful job even with me pacing and looking at my watch because we were running late, my fault, not Martin's. The ear to ear smile spread across my preteen's face when Martin was done was priceless. She was so thrilled when we walked out. Martin made a moment I pushed on my kid that could have ended in tears, fabulous. I adore Martin and my kid with the baby thin, stick straight hair was asked twice if she had clip ins or extentions. It was a miraculous job. The icing on the cake was when I called the next morning to ask for an email addres to thank Martin. Lisa remembered us and told me how happy my daughter's smile made them.
